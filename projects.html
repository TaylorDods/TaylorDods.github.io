Shell Eco-Marathon: Chassis

Our team designed and implemented a lightweight, energy-efficient chassis for the Shell Eco-Marathon competition, utilizing technologies such as SolidWorks, ANSYS, and python. Despite challenges integrating existing carbon fiber tubing with the vehicle shell and other components, we successfully created a structurally sound, modular design that was chosen for the competition.
